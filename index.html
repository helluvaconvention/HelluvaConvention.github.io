What is Helluva Con? Helluva Con is a convention revolving around all things Hellaverse (Hazbin Hotel and Helluva Boss). We are a fan convention, run by a very small team, and not associated with Vivziepop, Spindlehorse, Amazon, or any other existing entities. As this is our first time hosting a convention, we're planning a one day con, with the possibility for expansion in future years. We're working with a very small budget, so for now, we will be focusing on fan- created content and programming, rather than booking celebrity guests (although we hope to be able to do so in the future). We would appreciate it if you would take the time to fill out our interest form, so that we can get a better idea of what people would like to see at our con. Thank you! If you have any further questions, feel free to email us at helluvaconvention@gmail.com.
